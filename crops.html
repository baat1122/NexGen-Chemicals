Wheat (Gandum)

Gandum Pakistan ki aham tareen khazaak hai. Yellow rust (Peeley Rung ki Kangi) aur Jari Bootiyan (Wild Oats / Dumbi Sitta) yields ko barri hadd tak mutasir karti hain. Sowing se pehle seed treatment aur early growth stage par herbicide spray zaroori hai.

Rice (Chawal / Dhaan)

Dhaan ki fasal me stem borers (tanay ki sundi) aur leaf blast (paton ka jhulsaao) sab se baray khatray hain. Sucking insects ke khatmay ke liye timely spray aur micronutrient (chelated Zinc) application yield ko duna karti hai.

Sugarcane (Kamad / Ganna)

Cash crop sugarcane me deemak (termites) aur root borers zameen ke andar se nuksaan pohanchatay hain. Planting ke waqt liquid drenching aur early herbicides ka istemal booton ko mazbooti deta hai.

Orchards & Vegetables (Mango & Citrus)

Aam (mango) aur kinnow (citrus) ke baghaat me flowering se pehle stress aur insect attack thrips ko control karna zaroori hai. Amino acid bio-stimulants extreme temperature fluctuations ke khilaaf resistance paida kartay hain.
